// Background

10+ years building infrastructure others rely on

I am a Cloud Network Engineer with over 10 years of experience designing, deploying, and securing hybrid and cloud-native infrastructure, primarily on Microsoft Azure. My work spans automation, governance, identity, networking, and cybersecurity.

My career began in 2007 as a network administrator — working in budget-constrained environments where wearing many hats and getting things right the first time wasn't optional. That foundation shaped how I approach every infrastructure problem: understand every layer, secure from day one, automate everything repeatable.

Most recently I supported Alcoholics Anonymous through cloud transitions — mission-critical, high-trust environments where uptime and data security are non-negotiable. I help businesses scale securely and cost-effectively using Infrastructure-as-Code, Zero Trust principles, and advanced monitoring strategies.

I am currently building reusable cloud onboarding frameworks for small and mid-sized businesses (SMBs), helping them adopt secure cloud practices in fast-paced environments — and actively following the emergence of agentic AI and Copilot integration in cloud operations.
